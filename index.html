Getting to ArenaVille

ArenaVille 2019 is taking place within Arena Design Centre, located in Harringey Warehouse District.

The festival is a 15-minute walk from either Manor House tube station on the Piccadilly line, Seven Sisters station on the Victoria line, or Harringey Green Lanes and Stamford Hill stations on the London Overground lines. A number of bus services operate nearby and run at all hours.

Our Partners &  Collaborators



Zen Studio

ArenaVille 2019 is hosting the official opening of Zen Studio!

Zen Studio is a newly-opened music studio ready to welcome everyone who wants an atmospheric and homely space to find focus and inspiration.

A 4.7x3.5m fully-equipped studio offering hourly rental bookings including regular slots for individuals and bands, tailored for people who want to have everything ready for them when they turn up, ready to play.

Take some time on the day to visit the studio and talk to the team about bookings.
